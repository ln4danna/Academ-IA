**Project Overview**
Artificial Intelligence is transforming many areas of daily life such as education, health, industry, and entertainment. However, many people still do not fully understand how AI works or how it is applied.
This project presents AI concepts in an intuitive way through a responsive website that helps users: 
-Understand what Artificial Intelligence is
-Learn how it works at a basic level
-Explore real applications of AI in everyday life

**Objectives**
-Explain core AI concepts using simple and accessible language
-Provide interactive elements to support dynamic learning
-Show real-world applications of AI and its impact on society
-Encourage curiosity and informed adoption of AI technologies

**Features**
-Educational sections about Artificial Intelligence fundamentals
-Interactive content (quizzes, demonstrations, or visual explanations)
-Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices
-Clean and user-friendly interface

**Target Audience**
-Students
-Beginners interested in Artificial Intelligence
-Non-technical users curious about AI
-Educational communities

**Project Purpose**
This project was developed as an academic research initiative and as part of a personal portfolio, demonstrating skills in web development, UI/UX design, and educational technology.
